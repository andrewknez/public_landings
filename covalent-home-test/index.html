The problem

Your most important operational data lives on paper and in spreadsheets.

Industrial companies run on workforce capability. But training records sit in binders, skills live in Excel, and supervisors make assignment calls from memory. Covalent replaces the patchwork with one operational system of record.

Paper OJT records

Sign-offs in binders mean no real-time view of who is qualified, and audit prep takes weeks.

Excel skills matrices

Static spreadsheets are out of date the day they are built, with no link to actual work.

Tribal knowledge walking out the door

Retirements and churn drain expertise faster than new hires can absorb it.

One system to develop, plan, and deploy your workforce.

Three connected modules built around how industrial operations actually run, from on-the-job training to the moment a job is assigned.
