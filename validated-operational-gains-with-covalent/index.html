How the Covalent Platform works

Covalent's Platform includes deployment-specific functionality, meaning the tools to give frontline leaders a data-driven approach to job assignment. It matches the right people to the right work based on skills, availability, job priority, and historic efficiency, replacing manual matching and gut feel with AI-powered recommendations that leaders can review, adjust, and publish in minutes.

1

Prioritize Jobs

Rank the backlog for each zone with drag-and-drop prioritization, with critical job attributes surfaced in context for fast decisions.

2

Match People to Work

An AI matchmaking engine analyzes worker skills, certifications, availability, and past efficiency to recommend optimal assignments in seconds. Leaders fine-tune as needed.

3

Publish and Communicate

Push finalized plans instantly to shop floor displays or customer systems via API, so every worker knows their assignment the moment the shift starts.

Why the line needed a better way to assign work

  • 30+ engines in assembly at any given time
  • Variable progress across sub-modules, modules, and final assembly means every day brings a new plan
  • Frequent work stops plus demand backlog drive expensive overtime to catch up
  • Assigning work requires deep understanding of jobs plus each worker's efficiency and eligibility
  • Rich workforce data exists but cannot be readily put to use
